The Residence

Elevated interiors of Wood, Stone & Glass.

The great room rises beneath an exposed-beam vaulted ceiling, clad in warm cedar that draws the forest indoors. A stone hearth with a copper-mantled plaster fireplace anchors the room; expansive windows reveal the cedars on three sides.

The kitchen, updated in 2023, is set in book-matched quartz with a stainless apron sink, walk-in pantry, instant hot water, and Thermador appliances. A raised breakfast bar runs the length of the island; pendant lighting falls from the beams.

The Barn

A pavilion in the cedars.

Originally built in 2006 and converted in 2023, the detached barn is a fully finished pavilion: vaulted wood ceilings with skylights, two sets of nano doors that open to the lawn, and a mini-split for year-round comfort. Inside, a lounge with fireplace and wet bar; an exercise room; an office; storage; a half bath.

The square footage is not counted in MLS totals. It functions as a guest house, a studio, a third living room, depending on the day.
